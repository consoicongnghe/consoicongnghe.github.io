Phone tips

Android

  • camera click: inside system/media/audio/ui
  • clear passwords: data/system/locksettings.db*; gatekeeper.password.key; gatekeeper.pattern.key
  • clear fingerprints: /data/system/user/0/fpdata/user.db; /data/system/user/0/settings_fingerprint.xml
  • battery stat: /data/system/batterystats.bin

Nơi lưu pass wifi

  • data/etc/wifi
  • data/misc/dchp
  • data/misc/wifi

iOs

  • camera click: System/Library/Audio/UISounds/photoShutter.caf => rename to bak.
